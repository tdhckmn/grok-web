What is Grok?!

Grok?! is an adventure RPG game that emphasizes player ingenuity and creative problem-solving. Set in a gonzo world of limitless plausibility, players navigate challenges using wit, creativity, and unconventional thinking rather than traditional combat mechanics.

Whether you're exploring strange dimensions, solving impossible puzzles, or negotiating with bizarre entities, Grok?! encourages you to think outside the box and embrace the absurd.
